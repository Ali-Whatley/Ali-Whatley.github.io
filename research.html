Research

Current Projects

ASDL Grand Challenge: Energy Systems and National Security

Year-long group research project focused on creating tabletop tools to aid policymakers in making quantitative decisions for tactical responses to energy shocks. The project bridges systems engineering principles with international security analysis.

Pollitik - Political Popularity Project

Collaborative work with the Pollitik lab utilizing the Executive Approval Project database to facilitate comparative estimation of nations' electorates. Recent analysis includes:

• Jimmy Carter, Farmer, Politician, Humanitarian, Dies
• From North Korea with Love: North Korean Provocations and South Korean Rallies
• Presidential Approval in Ukraine, Past & Present

Past Research

An Examination of Natural Gas Deregulation

Semester-long economics research project examining natural gas pricing dynamics following deregulation of natural gas markets. Analysis used econometric methods to assess market efficiency and price formation mechanisms.
